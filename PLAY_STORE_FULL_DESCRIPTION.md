### **Full Description**

**Advisor Desk: The Ultimate Performance & Salary Tracker for WFH Advisors**

Stop guessing, start knowing. Advisor Desk is a powerful, offline-first productivity super-app designed exclusively for freelance and work-from-home (WFH) customer care advisors. Take complete control of your performance by tracking every metric that matters — from login hours and call counts to CSAT, CQ scores, and complex salary calculations — all in one beautifully designed, private, and secure app.

Powered by **Advisor Desk AI**, your personal performance coach, the app provides intelligent insights to help you hit your targets, forecast your earnings, and visualize your professional growth.

---

🚀 **Core Features**

✅ **Smart Work & Performance Tracking**
*   **Daily Logs:** Effortlessly log daily login hours (HH:MM:SS), total calls, and non-billable calls.
*   **Advanced Metrics:** Track customer satisfaction (CSAT) with T2/B2/N scores and monitor your Call Quality (CQ) audit scores.
*   **Leave Management:** Mark week-offs and personal leaves to maintain an accurate record of your work calendar.
*   **Full-Featured Calendar:** Visualize your entire month, including workdays, leaves, and performance, in an intuitive calendar view.

🧠 **Advisor Desk AI: Your Personal Coach**
*   **Proactive Insights:** Get daily, personalized tips and summaries on your dashboard — from morning briefings to end-of-day performance reviews.
*   **AI Performance Analyzer:** Get a deep-dive analysis of your monthly performance, with AI-generated feedback on your strengths and areas for improvement.
*   **AI Chat:** Have a conversation with your data. Ask questions in plain language like "How many more calls for my bonus?" or "What were my worst CSAT days last month?" and get instant answers.
*   **AI Goal Suggestions:** Let the AI analyze your past performance to suggest ambitious yet achievable monthly goals.

💰 **Intelligent Salary & Bonus Engine**
*   **Accurate Salary Forecasting:** Get a precise, real-time estimate of your monthly earnings.
*   **Detailed Breakdown:** See your salary calculated with complete transparency:
    *   Base Salary (based on billable calls)
    *   Performance Bonus (based on call & hour targets)
    *   CSAT Bonus (based on customer satisfaction scores)
    *   TDS Deduction
*   **Customizable Parameters:** Fine-tune all salary and bonus parameters in the settings to perfectly match your company's pay structure.

📊 **Advanced Goal Setting & Reporting**
*   **Set & Track Goals:** Define monthly targets for login hours and call counts and watch your progress in real-time with beautiful circular progress bars.
*   **Salary Projection:** See your potential earnings if you fully achieve your monthly goals.
*   **Customizable Reports:** Generate professional, well-structured reports for any date range. Choose exactly what to include — from daily entries to CSAT/CQ breakdowns and salary details.
*   **Export & Share:** Instantly export your reports as **PDF** or **Excel** files and share them via WhatsApp, Gmail, or any other app.

🔒 **Unmatched Privacy & Security**
*   **100% Offline-First:** Your data is stored securely on your device using a local database. No internet, no sign-up, no cloud storage.
*   **You Are in Control:** Your data is yours alone. We collect nothing.
*   **App Lock:** Secure your app with a **PIN** or **Biometric** (fingerprint/face) lock. Includes auto-lock timeout settings for added security.
*   **Backup & Restore:** Easily create a local backup of your entire database and restore it anytime, ensuring your data is always safe.

✨ **Polished & Customizable UI**
*   **Modern Interface:** A sleek, intuitive UI built with Flutter, featuring smooth animations and a clean user experience.
*   **Full Theme Control:**
    *   **Light & Dark Mode:** Choose your preferred mode or let it follow the system.
    *   **AMOLED Black Theme:** A true-black theme to save battery on OLED screens.
    *   **Material You:** Dynamic theming that adapts to your wallpaper (Android 12+).
    *   **Accent Colors:** Personalize the app with a variety of beautiful accent colors.
*   **Customizable Dashboard:** Reorder or hide dashboard widgets to create a layout that works for you.
*   **Home Screen Widget:** Keep track of your daily calls and CSAT score directly from your home screen (Android).
