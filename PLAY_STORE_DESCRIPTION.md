# Advisor Desk: Performance & Salary Tracker

Stop juggling spreadsheets and notebooks. Advisor Desk is the ultimate all-in-one productivity app for freelance and work-from-home (WFH) customer care advisors. Track your daily login hours, call counts, performance metrics, and instantly calculate your estimated salary—all in a secure, offline-first app.

Designed by an advisor, for advisors, this app empowers you to take control of your earnings. Visualize your progress towards monthly targets, understand your complete salary breakdown, and stay motivated to achieve your goals.

---

### **Key Features:**

**📊 Smart Performance Dashboard**
- Log daily login hours and calls handled with just a few taps.
- Track crucial quality metrics like CSAT (Customer Satisfaction) and CQ (Call Quality) scores.
- Automatically calculates your total working days and active hours.
- Easily edit or delete any entry to keep your records accurate.

**💰 Instant Salary Estimation**
- Get a detailed, automated breakdown of your monthly earnings:
  - **Base Salary** (based on call volume)
  - **Performance Bonus** (for achieving targets)
  - **CSAT & CQ Bonuses** (based on quality scores)
  - **TDS Deduction** & Final **Net Salary**
- Customize salary parameters in settings to match your pay structure perfectly.

**🎯 Motivational Goal Tracking**
- Set monthly goals for login hours and call counts.
- Visualize your progress in real-time with beautiful, animated circular progress bars.
- Stay focused and consistently hit your targets with a clear view of what's needed.

**📄 Professional PDF & Excel Reports**
- Instantly generate detailed performance reports for any date range.
- Customize reports to include only the sections you need.
- Export to both PDF and Excel for record-keeping or analysis.
- Share reports effortlessly via WhatsApp, Gmail, or any other app.

**🔒 Secure, Private & Offline-First**
- Your data is yours alone. All information is stored securely on your device's local database (SQFlite).
- **No Internet Required.** The app works perfectly offline.
- **No Sign-Up, No Cloud Sync.** Your privacy is our priority. We collect zero personal data.
- **100% Ad-Free.** No ads, trackers, or third-party analytics.

**✨ Modern & Smooth UI**
- A sleek and intuitive user interface built with Flutter for a fast, responsive experience.
- Choose between a beautiful Light or Dark mode.
- Clean, simple navigation designed for a focused workflow.

### **Who is Advisor Desk for?**

This app is specially crafted for:
- Freelance customer care advisors
- BPO & call center WFH agents
- Remote support agents on a target-based pay structure
- Anyone who needs to track calls, login hours, and complex salary metrics in a simple, unified way.

Whether you’re part of a DTH support campaign, e-commerce call center, or any remote voice process, this app helps you streamline your work and take control of your performance.

---

### **Privacy First. Always.**

Advisor Desk does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or performance data. Everything you log stays securely on your own device. No online account, login, or internet access is required. Your work history is yours and yours alone.
