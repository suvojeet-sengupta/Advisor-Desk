# Google Play Console Submission Answers

Here are the detailed answers to the questions for the Google Play Console submission.

---

### 1. How did you recruit users for your closed test?

We recruited testers directly from our target audience: customer service agents in India. This ensured feedback was highly relevant and that our app's logic was validated against real-world performance and salary structures. This focused approach guaranteed high-quality, actionable insights.

---

### 2. Describe the engagement you received from testers during your closed test.

Tester engagement was excellent. Users consistently used the app daily, testing all features from data entry to report generation. Their active feedback drove key improvements, such as a dedicated "Monthly Goals" section. This confirmed the app is intuitive and meets their daily needs.

---

### 3. Who is the intended audience for this app?

Our app is for customer service agents in India working for a specific satellite TV provider. They have a complex salary structure with performance bonuses (CSAT/CQ) and targets. Our app is a dedicated tool to help them track these metrics and accurately forecast their monthly earnings.

---

### 4. Describe how your app provides value to users?

The app empowers users by providing clarity and transparency over their complex earnings. It automates salary calculations, tracks performance against goals, and offers a detailed breakdown of their income. This eliminates manual work, motivates them, and helps them understand their financial standing.

---

### 5. What changes did you make to your app based on what you learned during your closed test?

Tester feedback was central to our development. We added customizable salary parameters, custom date-range reports, and data deletion options. We also enhanced the dashboard with more at-a-glance info cards and made significant UI/UX improvements based on their direct input for a better experience.

---

### 6. How did you decide that your app is ready for production?

The app is ready for production because it is stable, with no crashes reported by testers. All core features have been validated by our target audience, who confirm the calculations are accurate. Feedback has shifted from bug reports to positive affirmations, confirming the app is a valuable tool.
